Movement
--------

On Island:

	Left Analog Stick:		Move Robot in XZ-plane
	Left Trigger, A:		Activate Jetpack (->up)
	Press Right Analog Stick:	"Kick" island in desired direction (XZ-plane)
	Right Analog Stick + 
		Left Trigger, A:	Jump on nearest island in desired direction
	Right Analog Stick + 
		Right Trigger:		Attrackt island nearest island in desired direction
	
In Air:
	
	Left Analog Stick:		Move Robot in XZ-Plane
	Holding Left Trigger, A:	Keep jetpack active
	Releasing Left Trigger, A:	Deactivate jetpack
	
	
Actions
-------
	
	Y:				Flamethrower
	Right Trigger:			Ice spike
	Right Shoulder:			Hit
